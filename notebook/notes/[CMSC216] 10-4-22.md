Representing Data:



Characters:

-   ASCII
    -   Most common, stored in 8 bits with 7 being used for characters
-   EBCDIC
    -   8-bit code, used only by some IBM mainframes
-   UNICODE

-   There is imprecision that comes from representing infinite decimals (1/3) as finite bits
