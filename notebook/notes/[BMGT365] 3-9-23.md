V~t~= ((CF)~t~(1 + g)) / (r - g)
-   V~t~ = the value of the asset at time t
-   CF~t~ = the cash flow at time t expected to continue
-   g = the constant growth rate of cash flows in perpetuity
-   r = the appropriate risk adjusted discount rate
